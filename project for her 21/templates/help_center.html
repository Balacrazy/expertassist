Frequently Asked Questions

How do video sessions work?

Once an expert accepts your request, a dedicated session room is generated. You can securely join the room to initiate a WebRTC peer-to-peer video call and share screens.

What happens if no expert accepts?

Our Auto-Answer engine will automatically kick in after 20 minutes of inactivity, providing direct compiled resources from Google and YouTube tailored exactly to your request query.

How does the reporting system work?

If you receive poor service, you can report the session with proof. Automated security algorithms ban user accounts after sustained negative feedback thresholds to maintain quality.
